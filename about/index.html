About Us

Anna University was established in 1978 as a unitary type of university by bringing together the four reputed institutions in Chennai. The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) , UGC INDIA, accorded Anna University with a five star status. Today , it stands as one of the premier institutions of the country

About The Department

The department of chemical engineering was established in 1944 under the University of Madras. In. 1978, the department was transferred to the then newly formed Anna University. Department of chemical engineering is in its 75th year of excellence.



CHEMFLUENCE

Chemfluence is a National level technical symposium conducted by the Consortium of Chemical Technologists. Initially started as a college level symposium, Chemfluence has gained popularity over the years and now it has become one of the most anticipated technical symposiums in South India. Chemfluence 2019 will mark its 25th year of technical excellence with much more to offer to the students.
